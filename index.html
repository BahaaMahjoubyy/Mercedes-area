Mercedes, as a brand name, was created in 1901 by Austrian automotive engineer Emil Jellinek. He named the brand after his daughter, Mercedes Jellinek. The first Mercedes car was produced in 1902. Therefore, you could say that Mercedes, as a brand, was "born" in 1901 when it was officially registered, and the first Mercedes car was produced shortly afterward.
